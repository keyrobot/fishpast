What Exactly are Mutual Funds?

Before we get into the benefits, let’s clarify what mutual funds are. In simple terms, a mutual fund pools money from multiple investors to invest in a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, or other assets. Professional managers handle the investment decisions, so you don’t have to worry about choosing individual securities. The fund’s value fluctuates based on the performance of the assets inside it.
